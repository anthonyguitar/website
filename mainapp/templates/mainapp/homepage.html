My Current Project

Updated May 31, 2019
Description

This album is a mixture of instrumental rock and fusion. The creation process began in October 2018 when I rediscovered a passion for music after an almost 10 year dry spell. I am playing drums and guitar on all of the tracks. Quillen Blalock is playing piano, and Kyle Brashear is playing Bass. Ben Roehrs let me borrow a bass to write with so he is the true hero.

Release Date:

September 2019

Status:

Writing is done for 7/7 songs. Drums are finished for 7/7. Keys are finished for 2/7.

My Gear

Guitars
Suhr Guitars

This is my main guitar. I hardly touch any of the others in this list. It is the only guitar I am using on the Marshall On album. I first noticed Suhr when I saw some viral videos of Guthrie Govan on youtube (Fives I believe). Then I met Andy Wood (also from Knoxville) and he hooked me up with a deal on an amazing Suhr. Mine has an aqua-burst finish. It is basically a blue version of Andy's signature model at the time.

Drums
Roland

The electric kit is all I can play without my neighbors hating me. The GGD Modern and Massive Sample Pack actually makes electric drums a really option for recording.

Recording Hardware and Software
Macbook Pro

I went ahead and made sure to have 16GB of RAM. It holds up pretty well. Every now and then I'll need to put Logic into low latency mode while tracking. Additionally, sometimes I'll freeze tracks in order to lighten the load on the processor.

Fractal Audio

Aside from the Suhr, this is the best piece of equipment on the list. If I was stranded on an island and could only take two pieces of gear, they would be my Suhr and the Axe FX.

Logic Pro X

I'm on a Mac. So I use logic.

GGD

This sample pack provides a very cheap way to have amazing sounding drums. It is a must have if you do not have the means to track acoustic drums in an expensive studio.

Monitors

I like these monitors. Mine are really old and one of the two has static at certain frequencies, but I manage. They are FRFR speakers, so they are well suited to use as an amp for my Axe FX.
